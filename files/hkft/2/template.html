About Us

Why do we have such a strong desire to travel? It’s because we are always eager to hear the stories of others: every place you’ve visited, every person you’ve encountered, and every culture you’ve experienced have their unique stories. Do you truly understand the “Hong Kong Stories” beyond the major attractions?

Hong Kong Free Tours is committed to connecting travelers with Hong Kong stories by offering in-depth tours featuring diverse themes, enabling everyone to rediscover our city from a fresh perspective. We provide not only sightseeing day trips but also guide you to explore lesser-known locations, meet people who haven’t yet entered the public eye, and uncover the hidden Hong Kong stories behind the scenes.

If you’re eager to delve deeper into Hong Kong, or if you’re curious about non-mainstream and sub-cultural topics, come and join our tours! Together, let’s represent Hongkongers and share our unique Hong Kong stories.

TECHNICAL & OPERATION SUPPORT

As a nexus of finance and culture, Hong Kong represents a unique intersection where Eastern traditions blend with Western practices within a capitalist system adjacent to a communist regime, underscored by one of the world’s most pronounced Gini coefficients. Our theme tours are tailored to address a spectrum of compelling topics, ensuring relevance to your interests and compatibility with your academic calendar.

    Past themes have included:
  • Explorations of social inequality and injustice
  • The historical evolution of Hong Kong
  • Insights into the vanishing craft industries
  • Culinary journeys featuring both exotic and familiar tastes
  • Active excursions through Hong Kong’s landscapes, via hiking or biking
