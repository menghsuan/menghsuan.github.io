Hong Kong Free Tours - Leaflet Design

Graphic Design

2024

Overview:
This project involved the design and redesign of three leaflet sets for the Hong Kong Free Walking Tour Company. The aim was to enhance the promotional material for various walking tours by creating visually engaging designs that meet print requirements while delivering clear, impactful messaging to potential customers.

Dark Side of Hong Kong Walking Tour - Leaflet Design

Overview:
Complete redesign for this tour leaflet, including the front cover and content arrangement. The goal was to capture the gritty atmosphere of the tour while ensuring the leaflet remained professional and accessible.

Inspiration:
The layout was inspired by classic urban exploration designs, focusing on real-life experiences and media testimonials. The use of dark tones and highlighted accents emphasized the concept of exploring the lesser-known aspects of Hong Kong.

Tools Used: Illustrator, Photoshop

Introduction of Hong Kong Walking Tour - Leaflet Redesign

Overview:
Redesign of the leaflet's back side, including adjustments to the map layout and information flow. The design maintains the original bright and friendly aesthetic but restructures the information for a more organized and user-friendly experience.

Inspiration:
The design was inspired by a need to improve clarity for tourists, focusing on the landmarks and journey, while keeping a light and inviting color palette that reflects the welcoming nature of the tour.

Tools Used: Illustrator, Photoshop

Hong Kong Pub Crawl - Leaflet Redesign

Overview:
Updated the back of the leaflet to adjust the layout and improve the map’s visibility. The new design retained the bold, energetic color scheme that reflects the lively nightlife but made the information clearer and easier to follow.

Inspiration:
The redesign was inspired by vibrant nightlife posters, with a strong focus on enhancing the user experience and simplifying navigation through the night’s itinerary.

Tools Used: Illustrator, Photoshop
