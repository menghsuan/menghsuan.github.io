35th Graduation Concert (乘著音樂去旅行)

Graphic / Poster / Flyer / Brochure Design

2021

Overview:
Dunhua Elementary School's 35th Graduation Concert "乘著音樂去旅行" - Designed vibrant visual materials for concert & printed items to celebrate & enhance the event's visual identity.

Inspiration:
Concert theme "Traveling with Music" inspired the design. Sought to evoke a joyful music journey. Vibrant watercolors, musical notes, & landmark silhouettes represent the musical voyage. Bright colors & dynamic compositions reflect student energy & creativity.

Tools Used:
Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign

Invitation cards.

Entry passes.

A4 program booklets.
