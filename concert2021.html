35th Graduation Concert (乘著音樂去旅行)

Graphic / Poster / Flyer / Brochure Design

2021

Overview:
Designed comprehensive visual identity for Dunhua Elementary School's 35th Graduation Concert "乘著音樂去旅行" (Traveling with Music), creating cohesive branding across event materials and printed collateral.

Contributions:
• Developed the visual concept and key graphics based on the concert theme
• Designed posters, flyers, and event brochures for the graduation concert
• Created a consistent visual system across all promotional materials
• Prepared print-ready files for production

Design Approach:
The "Traveling with Music" theme inspired a vibrant visual language featuring watercolor textures, musical notes, and landmark silhouettes to represent a joyful musical journey. Bright color palettes and dynamic compositions were used to capture the energy and creativity of the students while creating memorable event branding.

Tools:
Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign

Invitation cards.

Entry passes.

A4 program booklets.
