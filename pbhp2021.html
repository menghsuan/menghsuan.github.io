Punk Baby House Party - NFT

Graphic Illustration

2021

Overview:
Contributed to the design and development of an NFT collection, creating character illustrations and establishing rarity systems for the ShelleyGen and MaryGen series.

Contributions:
• Designed unique character illustrations with distinct traits and attributes
• Developed rarity charts for ShelleyGen and MaryGen collections
• Created diverse visual elements to ensure collectibility and variation

Design Approach:
Drew inspiration from pop culture and vibrant aesthetics to create engaging, collectible characters. Each design incorporated unique trait combinations to build a diverse range appealing to NFT collectors while maintaining cohesive visual identity across the collection.

Tools:
Adobe Illustrator
